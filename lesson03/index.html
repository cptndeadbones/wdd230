Purpose:

The purpose of the Micro Burst Weather site is to inform locals of current weather conditions, warnings and news stories. This site will serve the user, the current local temp and give them emergency updates as well as a 7 day forcast. Users would also be informed on local events in the area. The site would be a place for local event planners of the community to post event and bulletins on activities to build the community. This weather site would work in conjunction with the local chaber of commerce to inspire commerce in the community.
