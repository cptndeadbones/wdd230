Purpose:

The purpose of Nimbus Weather site is to inform locals of specific towns about current weather conditions, warnings and news stories. This site will serve the user, the current local temp and give them emergency updates as well as a 7 day forcast. Users would also be informed on local events in the area. The site would be a place for local event planners of the community to post event and bulletins on activities to build the community.
